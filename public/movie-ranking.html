The best movies according to DiegoVolkx

My top 3 movies of all time

Infinity war

Until now it is my favorite superhero movie. It was the first movie I watched of Marvel, and the one that made me start watching that universe since the beginning. Amazing CGI, story, music, and just everything seems to be perfect.

Quote from the movie:

"I finally rest and watch the sun rise on a grateful universe.
The hardest choices require the strongest wills."

The Karate Kid

I love this movie. It is about a guy who is bullied and starts learning martial arts (karate specifically) from a Japanese man. Very inspiring

Quote from the movie:

"Man who catch fly with chopstick,
accomplish anything."

The Matrix

One of the best sci-fi movies of all time, in my opinion. It shows how the main character discovers that the world is just a simulation and everything around him is fake, and he is giving a choice to forget everything and live in ignorance, or escape from the matrix and experience the real world. You can easily draw out a very philosophical discussion from the movie.

Quote from the movie:

"The Matrix is everywhere.
It is all around us.
Even now, in this very room.
You can see it when you look out your window or when you turn on your television.
You can feel it when you go to work... when you go to church... when you pay your taxes.
It is the world that has been pulled over your eyes to blind you from the truth."
